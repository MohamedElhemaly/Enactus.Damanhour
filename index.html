About Enactus Damanhour University

Our Mission at Enactus

To enable progress through entrepreneurial action by engaging the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders to create a better, more sustainable world through Enactus Damanhour initiatives.

Enactus Vision

To become the leading student organization in Egypt that creates sustainable impact through entrepreneurial projects that transform lives and shape a better future through Enactus Damanhour University.

Enactus Values

Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, Passion, and Determination. We believe in the potential of human beings to make a positive difference through Enactus Damanhour.

Enactus Damanhour Projects

Student-led social entrepreneurship initiatives creating real impact in Egyptian communities

Saadany - Enactus Damanhour

Saadany empowers deaf and mute individuals by connecting them with real job opportunities. We bridge the gap to the labor market in sectors like manufacturing and hospitality with skill-building content. Our platform prepares them for employment and entrepreneurship.

18,000+ People Impacted
15 Villages Reached

Margosa - Enactus Damanhour

Margosa creates natural pesticides from neem extracts, offering farmers a safe, eco-friendly alternative to chemicals. Our solutions protect crops and soil health while supporting sustainable farming practices in Egypt.

84,000+ People Impacted
45 Businesses Launched

VetSea - Enactus Damanhour

VetSea protects cattle from parasites using natural algae-based treatments instead of chemicals. Our solution improves animal health and promotes sustainable livestock farming by replacing harmful drugs in Egyptian farms.

120+ Farmers Supported
35% Yield Increase

Ecoplast - Enactus Damanhour

Ecoplast tackles plastic waste by recycling it into new products like eco-friendly bricks. We empower communities by creating jobs and providing technical training, increasing workers' income by 350%.

600+ Jobs Created
350% Income Increase

Organalia - Enactus Damanhour

Organalia produces organic fertilizer from Nile rose and animal waste to reduce environmental impact. We empower communities by creating jobs and providing technical training.

300+ Lives Saved
27 Village Reached

Brickstick - Enactus Damanhour

Brickstick transforms plastic waste into eco-friendly bricks, offering a sustainable construction material. We partner with factories to scale production in Egypt.

80,000+ Plastic Bricks
160+ Families Impacting

Mushraws - Enactus Damanhour

Mushraws transforms rice straw waste into valuable products like mushrooms, animal feed, and organic fertilizer. Our zero-waste model prevents harmful burning in Egyptian farms.

230% Income Increase
210 Families Impacting

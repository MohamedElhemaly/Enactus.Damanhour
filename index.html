About Enactus Damanhour

Our Mission

To enable progress through entrepreneurial action by engaging the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders to create a better, more sustainable world.

Our Vision

To become the leading student organization in Egypt that creates sustainable impact through entrepreneurial projects that transform lives and shape a better future.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, Passion, and Determination. We believe in the potential of human beings to make a positive difference.

Our Projects

Student-led initiatives creating real impact in our community

Saadany

Saadany empowers deaf and mute individuals by connecting them with real job opportunities. We bridge the gap to the labor market in sectors like manufacturing and hospitality with skill-building content. Our platform prepares them for employment and entrepreneurship.

18,000+ People Impacted
15 Villages Reached

Margosa

Margosa creates natural pesticides from neem extracts, offering farmers a safe, eco-friendly alternative to chemicals. Our solutions protect crops and soil health while supporting sustainable farming practices.

84,000+ People Impacted
45 Businesses Launched

VetSea

VetSea protects cattle from parasites using natural algae-based treatments instead of chemicals. Our solution improves animal health and promotes sustainable livestock farming by replacing harmful drugs.

120+ Farmers Supported
35% Yield Increase

Ecoplast

Ecoplast tackles plastic waste by recycling it into new products like eco-friendly bricks. We empower communities by creating jobs and providing technical training, increasing workers' income by 350%.

600+ Jobs Created
350% Income Increase

Organalia

Organalia produces organic fertilizer from Nile rose and animal waste to reduce environmental impact. We empower communities by creating jobs and providing technical training.

300+ Lives Saved
27 Village Reached

Brickstick

Brickstick transforms plastic waste into eco-friendly bricks, offering a sustainable construction material. We partner with factories to scale production.

80,000+ Plastic Bricks
160+ Families Impacting

Mushraws

Mushraws transforms rice straw waste into valuable products like mushrooms, animal feed, and organic fertilizer. Our zero-waste model prevents harmful burning.

230% Income Increase
210 Families Impacting

About Enactus Damanhour

Our Mission

To enable progress through entrepreneurial action by engaging the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders to create a better, more sustainable world.

Our Vision

To become the leading student organization in Egypt that creates sustainable impact through entrepreneurial projects that transform lives and shape a better future.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, Passion, and Determination. We believe in the potential of human beings to make a positive difference.

Our Projects

Student-led initiatives creating real impact in our community

Saadany

Saadany empowers deaf and mute individuals by connecting them with real job opportunities. We bridge the gap to the labor market in sectors like manufacturing and hospitality with skill-building content. Our platform prepares them for employment and entrepreneurship. We have directly improved the lives of over 18,840 people.

18,000+ People Impacted
15 Villages Reached

Margosa

Margosa creates natural pesticides from neem extracts, offering farmers a safe, eco-friendly alternative to chemicals. Our solutions protect crops and soil health while supporting sustainable farming practices. We empower local farmers with accessible, low-cost alternatives for crop protection. Our work has directly impacted 2,600 people and indirectly benefited 84,000 more.

84000+ People Impacted
45 Businesses Launched

VetSea

VetSea protects cattle from parasites using natural algae-based treatments instead of chemicals. Our solution improves animal health and promotes sustainable livestock farming by replacing harmful drugs. This has increased milk production by 17% and meat production by 15%. We have also reduced reliance on chemical medications by 40%.

120+ Farmers Supported
35% Yield Increase

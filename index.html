About Enactus Damanhour

Our Mission

To enable progress through entrepreneurial action by engaging the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders to create a better, more sustainable world.

Our Vision

To become the leading student organization in Egypt that creates sustainable impact through entrepreneurial projects that transform lives and shape a better future.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, Passion, and Determination. We believe in the potential of human beings to make a positive difference.
